Nikujaga

Nikujaga - A delicoius Japanese meat and potato stew!

Nikujaga is a Japanese dish that literally means "meat and potatoes" in English. It is a type of stew made with sliced beef, potatoes, onions, and carrots cooked in a sweetened soy sauce and mirin (a sweet rice wine) broth. Other ingredients such as shirataki noodles, green beans, and mushrooms may also be added depending on the recipe. Nikujaga is a popular comfort food in Japan and is often served as a main dish with rice and miso soup.
